  • NiceKick

    NiceKick is an app for soccer players to keep a track of their practice sessions and record personalized data on their performance.

    NiceKick is an application that is designed for soccer players to record their training sessions so that they can record stats and data on their shooting accuracy.

    The app will allow them to keep sessions stored in an organized manner, but also clearly show areas of improvement in their game.

    As a former soccer player and coach I did not find an app that was easy to use and store data for myself or my players that I could present data in a clear and concise way.
